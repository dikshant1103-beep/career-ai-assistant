Job Title: Battery Data & ML Engineer
Company: VoltCell Energy

About the role
We are hiring a Battery Data & ML Engineer to build the next generation of
battery analytics for our electric two-wheeler fleet. You will work on
state-of-charge (SoC), state-of-health (SoH), and remaining-useful-life
(RUL) models on data from thousands of vehicles operating in real-world
conditions.

Responsibilities
- Design and train ML models for SoC / SoH / RUL on time-series battery telemetry.
- Build ETL pipelines from telematics into a feature store.
- Combine physics-based electrochemical models (PyBaMM / SPM) with data-driven
  approaches for hybrid SoC / RUL estimation.
- Productionize models for on-device deployment in battery management systems.
- Collaborate with vehicle dynamics and BMS firmware teams.

Required skills
- Strong Python (PyTorch, NumPy, scikit-learn).
- Experience with time-series modelling (LSTM / Transformer / SSM / Mamba).
- Familiarity with electrochemical models (PyBaMM / DFN / SPM).
- Strong fundamentals in linear algebra, probability and ODEs.
- Git, Docker, Linux.

Preferred
- PINNs / physics-informed ML.
- C++ for on-device inference (LibTorch).
- Experience with two-wheeler / EV powertrain data.
- Publications in battery ML, conference papers welcome.

Location: Hybrid, Bengaluru. Open to recent graduates and final-year
students with relevant publications or strong projects.
