Job Title: Vehicle Dynamics & Simulation Engineer (Two-Wheeler)
Company: ApexMoto Engineering

Role overview
You will build and validate the multibody-dynamics (MBD) simulation stack
that powers our motorcycle chassis development. The role bridges classical
vehicle dynamics, modern simulation tooling, and product design.

Responsibilities
- Develop and maintain multibody simulation models (PyChrono / Adams) of
  motorcycle chassis, suspension and tires.
- Implement and validate Pacejka Magic Formula tire models including
  combined slip, Mz, and contact-patch migration.
- Couple MBD with FEM (ANSYS) for stiffness / NVH workflows.
- Build CAD-driven parameter sweeps (SolidWorks / NX) and automate them.
- Correlate simulation results with rig and track data.
- Mentor junior engineers on chassis fundamentals.

Required
- Strong vehicle dynamics fundamentals (yaw, roll, gyroscopic effects).
- Python (NumPy, SciPy) AND C++.
- Hands-on with PyChrono / Adams / Simpack OR equivalent MBD tool.
- FEM experience (ANSYS / Abaqus).
- CAD: SolidWorks or NX.

Preferred
- Pacejka tire modelling expertise.
- Custom integrators (Generalized-alpha / RK4) for stiff MBD.
- ML for surrogate modelling.

Compensation: competitive + equity. Location: Pune, India.
